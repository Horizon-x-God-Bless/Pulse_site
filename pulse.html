FAQ

Got Questions?

How do I place an order? +
Simply click the "Order Now" button on any product and you'll be redirected to our WhatsApp where you can confirm your order with one of our team members.
How much deposit do I need to pay? +
A deposit of 30–50% of the product price is required to confirm your order. The balance is paid on delivery. We accept Airtel Money and MTN Mobile Money.
Do you deliver outside Lusaka? +
Yes! We can ship nationwide via bus or courier. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact us on WhatsApp for details.
Are the refurbished laptops reliable? +
Absolutely. All refurbished laptops are thoroughly tested and graded before sale. We provide a description of the condition and specs so you know exactly what you're getting.
What if I receive a faulty product? +
Contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll either replace the item or issue a refund depending on the situation. Customer satisfaction is our priority.
Can I request a product not listed? +
Yes! Chat with us on WhatsApp and we'll try to source it for you. We take custom orders and can often get products within a few days.
